**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/jasonmlemkin_one-reason-its-very-hard-for-b2b-companies-activity-7470931625145692160-AJAV
**Posted:** June 2026
**Reactions:** 65

One reason it's very hard for B2B companies to compete with Claude: everything hard costs $1 or so.

I'm simplifying, but a very simple B2B AI action in Anthropic or OpenAI API might cost pennies if you can use Haiku or Mini (the cheap, simple, fast models).

But those long, complex analyses of documents, financials, etc.? The ones that sort of look like Claude… because in large part they are?

That API call with a lot of compute is expensive. It's often $1.00. $0.50 if you really optimize.

And your end users and customers can do a lot of it in Claude as part of their existing subscriptions.

A B2B vendor doing a truly complex AI analysis might pay effectively 10x what Anthropic charges its own customers.

It's tough for B2B vendors to then mark that up 2x or more to make it profitable.

So they end up, in many cases, with pretty mediocre AI implementations.
