Today we're publishing Lovable's first ever data study! A few stats worth paying attention to:

- 80% of builders come from non-technical backgrounds
- 55% have 11+ years of professional experience
- 79% are building something they intend to monetize
- 35% are already earning revenue
- The strongest predictor of what someone builds is what they already do and know
- The fastest-growing builder groups are founders, designers, and sales professionals
- Top categories of apps built: websites & landing pages, business operations & mgmt, consumer apps, dashboards & analytics

Not every stat is where we want it to be yet - there's still a big gender gap - and that's exactly why initiatives like SheBuilds matter. More people should see themselves as builders, and more people should feel like they have permission to start.

But overall, the category is moving from people randomly experimenting with AI tools to experienced people turning their knowledge, problems, workflows, and customer insight into real software. LFG!
